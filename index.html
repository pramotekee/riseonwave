Retreat Flow

5-Day Reset Journey

🌅 Day 1: Arrival & Intention

Arrive at the retreat center, settle in, set your intention for the journey ahead. Welcome circle and orientation.

🌊 Day 2: Ocean Connection

Morning surf session to connect with the ocean. Learn to read waves and understand the rhythm of the sea.

🏄 Day 3: Flow & Balance

Advanced surf coaching focusing on flow state. Balance work on land and in water.

🌅 Day 4: Release & Let Go

Morning meditation by the ocean. Surf session focused on letting go of control and trusting the wave.

🌴 Day 5: Integration & Return

Final surf session. Closing circle, sharing reflections, and integrating lessons before departure.
